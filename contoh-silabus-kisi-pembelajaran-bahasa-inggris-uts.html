13. TOLONG BANTU JAWAB BUAT KISI KISI UTS BESOK


a. Busur = a/360 x 2.phi.r
= 90/360 . 2 . 22/7 . 28
= 44 cm

b. Juring = a/360.phi.r^2
= 90/360 . 22/7 . 28^2
= 616 cm^2

c. Luas yg diarsir (tembereng) = luas juring AOB - segitiga AOB

Luas segitiga AOB = 1/2.28.28
= 392 cm^2

Tembereng = juring - segitiga
= 616 - 392
= 224 cm^2

luas2=lingkaran

L1=s²
=14²
=196cm²

s=d=2r
r=14/2=7

L2=πr²
=22/7×7×7
=22×7
=154cm²

Ltotal=L1+L2
=196+154
=350cm²
