38. Sebutkan 5 contoh asking for a promise


Answer:

When you ask for promises, you tend to ask someone to say the expression of promises under pressure, not voluntarily. Such kind of question is normally uttered when you insist to have the other party's word as a warranty or you worry that it can't be accomplished. Here are some example of asking for promises.

1. Will you promise me for that?

2. Do you promise that you will do that?

3. Promise me first for that, would you?

4. May I have your word for such matter?

5. I will have your word for that, OK?

Hopefully those are sufficient.

Mapel: English

Kategori: question of promises
