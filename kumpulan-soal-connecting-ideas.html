26. apa yang dimaksud dengan stated main ideas


The "main idea" of a paragraph is its "point" or overall message. A "stated main idea" is a "main idea" that is stated directly - you don't have to guess or infer it. 
Example: 
I like cats. They're so cute and fuzzy. They make adorable sounds when you pet them in interesting ways. Sometimes they bite you if you tickle their bellies, but I like that, too. 
"I like cats" would be the stated main idea. You could have inferred that the author liked cats without that sentence, but it's less work for the reader when they're simply told what the paragraph is about.
