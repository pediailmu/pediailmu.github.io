28. explain what you know about subject verb and pronoun agrament​


Explanation:

Short;

Subject-verb agreement simply means that the subject and verb in a sentence must agree in number. They both need to be singular, or they both need to be plural.

Long; Subject-Verb Agreement Examples

Subject-verb agreement means that the sentence is grammatically correct. Subject-verb agreement is usually more of a problem in present-tense sentences. Verbs in the past tense don't change as much when the number of the subjects change ("He walked" is the same verb form as "They walked," for example).

Here are some examples of subject-verb agreement in singular subjects and verbs (the subject is bolded and the verb underlined):

My dog waits for the postal carrier.I understand the assignment.Peter likes vegetables.

Subject-verb agreement examples with plural subjects and verbs include:

Basketballs roll across the floor.These clothes are too small for me.The cats meow at the door.Compound Subjects

Tentukan subject dan verb dari kalimat - kalimat dibawah ini!

1. They did not take train to got to Jogja

subject : They

verb : did not take

2. I met my old friend yesterday

subject : I

verb : met

3. We went to cinema last night

subject : We

verb : went

4. Did he drink the milk last night?

subject : He

verb : drink

5. My sister did not wash the dishes

subject : My sister

verb : did not wash

Pembahasan

Simple past tense : Menjelaskan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lalu.

Rumus simple past tense :

(+)  Subject + verb 2 + object

(-) Subject + did not + base verb + object

(?) Did + subject + base verb + object

Examples :

(+) He cried over the milk

(-) He did not cry over the milk

(?) Did he cry over the milk?

(+) She bought me an expensive gift

(-) She did not buy me an expensive gift

(?) Did she buy you an expensive gift?
