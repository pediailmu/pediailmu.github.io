14. When saat bahasa ingris. Dipaki saat apa? Dan while juga di gunakan pada saat apa?


⇒ Kata when berfungsi sbg conjunction yg digunakan utk kejadian singkat, jadi when digunakan pd dua peristiwa yg berurutan, dan when biasanya diikuti dgn tense non progressive (non continuous) tapi when bisa diikuti verb-ing Gerund.
Contoh: 
I was studying English when my friend came
⇒ Kata while jg berperan sbg conjunction dan while digunakan utk kejadian yg terjadi secara bersamaan,  jika when biasanya selalu diikuti tense non progressive maka while sebaliknya, while selalu diikuti oleh kata kerja+ing atau tense progressive (continuous).
Contoh:
Please, don't disturb him while he's studying
when itu kapan
while itu sementara

36. Buatkan description tentang telor congkel menggunakan bahasa ingris


Jawaban:

Scrambled eggs, commonly referred to as "telor congkel" in Indonesian, is a delectable dish prepared by whisking eggs and cooking them in a skillet. The eggs are typically beaten until they achieve a uniform consistency, and then they are gently cooked over medium heat with occasional stirring. As the eggs begin to set, they form soft, delicate curds.

"Telor congkel" is a versatile dish that can be customized with various seasonings and ingredients like salt, pepper, herbs, cheese, or vegetables to enhance its flavor. It's often served for breakfast or brunch and pairs wonderfully with toast or as a filling for sandwiches. The result is a creamy, fluffy, and satisfying egg dish that is enjoyed by people of all ages.
