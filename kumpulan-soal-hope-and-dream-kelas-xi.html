14. rumus suggestion and offering , hope and dream and examples please :D


suggestion : subject + verb + that + object
(i propose that a report should be sent in)
offering : modal verb + subject + object
(shall we take you there)
hope : subject + verb + object/complement
(i hope rahmad found the place)

17. Contoh dari expressing hope and dream itu apa?


Hope (Harapan) ﻿(menggunakan present tense)
- I hope I can win the competition.
- I hope my mother get well soon.
- I hope she is okay.
- I want to become a doctor.
- I hope she accepts my feeling.

Dream (Mimpi) ﻿(menggunakan past tense)
- I wish you were here.
- I wish I had studied harder in high school.
- I wish I could fly.
- If only I knew how to cook.
- I wish I were a millionaire.

29. Contoh dari expressing hope and dream


kategori : Bahasa Inggris - hopes and dreams
materi : SMA XI

pembahasan:
unsur kebahasaan dalam hopes and dreams expression adalah:
1) using "-ing verb":
Subject + -ing verb + Complement
I am hoping for better future next year.

2) using "to" and "that":
Subject + verb + Complement
I hope to have a memorable journey.
I hope to know where the address is.
I hope that i will be success.
I hope that i will be a doctor.

3) using verb tenses:
Subject + verb + Complement/Object
I hope she finished her project soon.
I hope you find your lost key.

40. Write in good paragraph about your hope and dream in 2021


My dream and hope on 2O21 !

Everyone has wonderful hopes and dreams of their future. As a typical Virgo, I extremely desire everything be perfect, especially my future life. I want to be a successful woman in the ne

The meaning of success is quite different for everyone. For me, the first obvious thing is a good career. I hope I can run my own business and make it to be international in order to earn more money for myself and my family. As a result of being wealthy, I can obtain a higher life standard and it also makes me have the sense of personal achievement.

However, being successful is not only means to be rich. I firmly believe that liberty is vital importance of my life. Therefore, I am going to do want I want to do. I do not want to be too busy to spend time with families and friends. I need to enjoy my free time to have parties and family gatherings. Moreover, I am keen to learn different languages; I wish I can have enough time to learn new languages, which can help me to open my mind by communicating with people all over the world and to enrich my life.

Another consideration of being successful is a happy family. I deeply hope I can find my Mr. Right in 2021. We will live in a big house with beautiful garden and swimming pool and filled with love. We can raise our children together and get older together. We can work hard during the day and travel around the world in vacation.

I strongly think all of these hopes are not a dream. I have the confidence to make these come true through my unremitting effort.

And i hope in these 2021, corona virus is gone !
