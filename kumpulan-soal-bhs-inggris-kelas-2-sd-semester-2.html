Pembahasan

1. One sixtieth of a minute: second

2. 60 seconds: minute

3. 60 minutes: hour

4. Tells time & hangs on the wall: clock

5. Timepiece worn on the wrist: wristwatch

6. Number of hours in a day: 24 hours

7. Shows the seconds: second hand

8. Slowest hand on a clock: hour hand

9. Shows the minutes: minute hand

10. Keeps watch attached to wrist: strap or band

11. One o’clock: 1:00

12. One fifty-five: 1:55

13. Midnight: 12:00 A.M.

14. Noon: 12:00 P.M.

15. A.M.: 12:00 P.M to 11:59 A.M.

16. 1:05: one-oh-five

17. 2:10: ten after two

18. 4:30: four-thirty

19. 5:45: a quarter to six

20. 3:45: three forty-five

39. tolong yang bisa dijawab yaa buat besok kelas 7 semester 2 yang bisa bhs Inggris nanti aku follow


1. (-) Linda does not visit her grandma every week.

2. (?) Does Arman play football on Saturday?

3. (-) The students do not have English lesson twice a week.

4. (+) My mother goes shopping on weekend.

5. (?) Does my father work on Fridays?

6. (+) The children watch TV in the evening.

7. (+) Mrs. Anita teaches Mathematic on Tuesday.

8. (-) Mr. Arif does not read newspaper every morning.

9. (+) Your grandpa and grandma listen to the music every day.

10. (-) Your friends do not study the lesson every night.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Semua kalimat di atas menggunakan rumus kalimat Simple Present Tense. Perhatikan rumus berikut ini!

(+ / Positive) Subject + Verb 1/(Verb -s/-es) + Object + Signal word

(- / Negative) Subject + do not/does not + Verb 1 + Object + Signal word

(? / Interrogative) Do/Does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + Signal word?

*Signal words: every night, every day, in the evening, etc.

Pembahasa SIMPLE PRESENT, rumus nya :
(+) subject + V1 (s/es) + object
(-) subject + Do/does + not + V1 + object
(?) Do/does + subject + V1 + object

Catatan !!!!
* Tambahan s/es pada kalimat (+) hanya untuk orang ketiga tunggal, seperti : she/he/it/dsb.
* Auxiliary verb DO digunakan hanya untuk subject I/you/they/we/plural subject.
* Auxiliary verb DOES digunakan hanya untuk subject she/he/it/orang ketiga tunggal.


Jawaban :
1). (-) Linda DOES NOT VISIT her grandma every week.
2). (?) DOES Arman PLAY football on Sundays?
3). (-) The students DO NOT HAVE English lesson twice a week.
4). (+) My mother GOES shopping on weekend.
5). (?) DOES my father WORK on fridays?
6). (+) The children WATCH Tv in the evening
7). (+) Mrs. Anita TEACHES Mathematic on Tuesdays.
8). (-) Mr. Arif DOES NOT READ newspaper every morning.
9). (+) Your grandpha and grandma LISTEN to the music every day.
10). (-) your friends DO NOT STUDY the lesson every night.
